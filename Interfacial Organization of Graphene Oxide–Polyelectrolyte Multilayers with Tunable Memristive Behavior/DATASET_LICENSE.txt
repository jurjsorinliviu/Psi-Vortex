Use in this repository
----------------------
A SUBSET of this dataset is redistributed here: only the current-voltage sweep
files (IV-plot_*.xlsx), unchanged. These are the files used by the real-data
validation experiments (see data_go_pe.py and r1_real_data_validation.py). The
complete dataset (AFM, QCM-D, UV-VIS, DLS, and potentiation-depression data) is
available unchanged at the figshare DOI above; download it from there to
reproduce analyses that need those files.
